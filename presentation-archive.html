01 / Overview

A record of rooms that remain ready.

Everything Is Happening Without You is a widescreen British art-rock record about helpful systems, ordinary places and the moment a person becomes less necessary than the infrastructure around them.

The world remains lit, heated, open and fully maintained. The roads continue. The televisions continue. The instructions remain clear. Nothing has failed.
